Braze - AI in a CDP landscape

How is the CDP moving ahead with AI?...

In today's digital landscape, understanding and engaging customers effectively is paramount for businesses. Braze, a leading customer engagement platform, has been at the forefront of this endeavor, continually evolving its offerings to meet the dynamic needs of modern marketers. A significant milestone in this evolution is the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) into their Customer Data Platform (CDP), enhancing the way brands interact with their audiences.

Introduction to Braze's Customer Data Platform

Braze's CDP is designed to unify first-party customer data, providing brands with a holistic view of their audience's behaviors and preferences. By integrating data from various touchpoints—such as mobile apps, websites, and in-store interactions—Braze enables personalized and timely customer engagements. This unified approach not only streamlines marketing efforts but also ensures that communications are relevant and resonate with individual customers. The platform's ability to seamlessly integrate with other technologies further amplifies its effectiveness, allowing brands to derive actionable insights and execute campaigns with precision.

The Braze Capability...

Braze is widely used by businesses across the globe, spanning industries such as retail, finance, entertainment, hospitality, and more. With a strong presence in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, Braze powers customer engagement for some of the world’s leading brands, including HBO, Burger King, Etsy, and Grubhub. Companies leverage Braze’s AI-enhanced Customer Data Platform to create personalized, real-time customer interactions that drive engagement and loyalty. By unifying first-party data and automating messaging across multiple channels—email, push notifications, in-app messaging, and SMS—Braze enables brands to deliver highly targeted and relevant experiences. This results in increased customer retention, improved marketing efficiency, and ultimately, higher revenue.

  • For businesses, the ability to make data-driven decisions at scale while maintaining a personalized touch is a game-changer, positioning Braze as a leader in the customer engagement space.
  • Global Reach: Braze serves over 1,000 brands across more than 50 countries, engaging with over 9 billion global users through its customer engagement platform.
  • Company Size Distribution: Among Braze's customers, approximately 53% are small businesses with fewer than 50 employees, 31% are medium-sized businesses, and 14% are large enterprises with over 1,000 employees.

    • Braze Evolving with AI

    The infusion of AI into Braze's CDP has ushered in a new era of customer engagement, marked by automation, personalization, and efficiency. Key AI-powered enhancements include:

    AI-Generated Content Creation

    Braze's AI tools assist marketers in crafting compelling content. Features like the BrazeAI Liquid Assistant and Message Template Assistant utilize generative AI to help develop advanced personalization logic and sophisticated message templates through simple text prompts.


    Predictive Analytics

    By analyzing historical and real-time data, Braze's AI capabilities can forecast customer behaviors, such as purchase likelihood or potential churn. This foresight allows brands to proactively address customer needs, optimize retention strategies, and enhance overall satisfaction.

    Personalized Recommendations

    Leveraging AI, Braze enables brands to deliver tailored product or content recommendations to individual users. This personalization enhances user experience, increases engagement, and drives conversions by presenting customers with items that align with their preferences and behaviors.



    Enhanced Segmentation

    AI-driven segmentation allows for the creation of more precise and dynamic audience groups based on nuanced patterns and behaviors. This ensures that marketing campaigns are highly targeted, relevant, and effective, leading to improved engagement metrics.

    Real-Time Data Processing

    With AI, Braze's CDP can process and analyze vast amounts of data in real-time, enabling immediate insights and timely customer interactions. This agility ensures that brands can respond to customer actions as they happen, fostering a more connected and responsive relationship.

    Braze - Future Direction...

    Looking ahead, Braze aims to deepen its AI capabilities to further enhance customer engagement. This includes developing more sophisticated predictive models, expanding automation features, and enhancing integration with other AI-driven tools. The goal is to empower brands to deliver even more personalized, efficient, and impactful customer experiences.

    Braze is actively advancing its platform to enhance personalized, data-driven customer engagement. At its annual conference, Forge 2024, Braze unveiled several innovations designed to empower marketers. Notably, the company introduced Project Catalyst, an AI-driven agent that enables marketers to craft individualized experiences at scale. By inputting high-level guidelines, marketers can leverage Project Catalyst to generate numerous personalized content variants, optimizing subject lines, messaging tones, offers, channel mixes, and timing for each consumer. This initiative underscores Braze's commitment to integrating artificial intelligence to streamline and enhance marketing strategies. ​

    In addition to AI advancements, Braze is expanding its global footprint to better serve a diverse clientele. The company has announced plans to establish local teams, appoint in-market leadership, and open new offices in regions such as Brazil, Bucharest, Dubai, and Seoul. This strategic expansion aims to provide tailored support and foster stronger relationships with clients worldwide, reflecting Braze's dedication to meeting the unique needs of various markets.

    ​Furthermore, Braze continues to prioritize inclusivity and support for underrepresented groups in the tech industry. The company's Tech for an Equitable Future program has recently welcomed 17 new companies, offering them 12 months of free access to the Braze platform along with comprehensive support services. This initiative aims to accelerate the growth of these startups and increase their chances of securing venture funding, highlighting Braze's commitment to fostering equity and inclusion within the technology sector.

    Real-World Application - Pizza Hut

    In the competitive world of quick-service restaurants (QSR), Pizza Hut recognized the need to modernize its customer engagement strategies to stay ahead. The goal was clear: enhance communication channels, personalize customer interactions, and drive revenue growth.​

  • The Challenge: Outdated Communication Systems - Prior to partnering with Braze, Pizza Hut's communication platforms were limited and inflexible. Their existing system lacked native mobile channels and a unified view of cross-channel customer communications, making timely and relevant engagement a challenge.​

  • The Strategy: Integrating Braze for Cross-Channel Personalization - Pizza Hut integrated Braze into their marketing ecosystem to revamp their approach.

  • Multi-Channel Messaging: Implemented email, SMS, push notifications, and in-app messaging to reach customers effectively.
    • ​
  • Machine Learning Optimization: Utilized Braze's capabilities to deploy a multi-armed bandit (MAB) machine learning algorithm, enabling real-time optimization of email campaigns.​

  • Personalized Content: Leveraged Braze's dynamic content features to tailor messages based on customer behavior and preferences.​

    • The collaboration with Braze led to remarkable outcomes:​

  • 21% Increase in Revenue: Personalized campaigns contributed to a significant boost in sales.​
  • 30% Increase in Transactions: Enhanced engagement strategies resulted in more customer purchases.​
  • 10% Increase in Profit: Optimized marketing efforts led to improved profitability.​

    • These results underscore the power of personalized, data-driven customer engagement in driving business growth.​

    Key 3 takeouts...

    • Real-Time, Cross-Channel Customer Engagement - Braze empowers brands to deliver personalized messages across multiple channels—including email, SMS, push notifications, and in-app messaging—by leveraging real-time data. This ensures timely and relevant communication that enhances customer engagement and retention.

    • AI-Powered Optimization and Personalization - Incorporating advanced AI and machine learning algorithms, Braze optimizes message timing, channel selection, and content personalization. Features like Intelligent Timing and Intelligent Channel help ensure that messages reach users when and where they're most likely to engage, improving overall campaign effectiveness.

    • Seamless Integration and Scalability - Designed for flexibility, Braze integrates easily with leading technology platforms, allowing brands to scale their customer engagement efforts efficiently. Its robust infrastructure supports rapid deployment and growth, enabling companies to build and maintain strong customer relationships as they expand.

      • These attributes make Braze a powerful solution for businesses aiming to enhance their customer engagement strategies through personalized, data-driven communication.
